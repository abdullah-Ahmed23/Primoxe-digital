Getting Started

Getting started is simple:

Our Process

The timeline depends on the scope of work, but here's a general breakdown:

  • Discovery & Research: 3-5 days
  • Strategy Development: 5-7 days
  • Design & Development: 14-21 days
  • Launch & Optimization: Ongoing

Most comprehensive brand builds take 4-6 weeks from start to launch. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our consultation based on your specific needs.

Our process follows four main phases:

1. Discovery & Research

We learn everything about your business, industry, and goals through interviews, market research, and competitive analysis.

2. Strategy Development

We create a custom brand strategy including positioning, messaging, visual identity plan, and marketing strategy.

3. Design & Execution

We bring your brand to life with professional design and development of all assets (logo, website, marketing materials, etc.).

4. Launch & Optimization

We launch your brand and continuously monitor performance, making data-driven improvements.

See our How It Works page for more details.

We design our process to be collaborative but not overwhelming. Your involvement includes:

  • Initial discovery sessions (1-2 hours total)
  • Strategy approval (review and feedback)
  • Design feedback (typically 2-3 rounds)
  • Final review and launch

We handle all the heavy lifting—you just need to provide timely feedback at key milestones. Most clients spend 2-4 hours per week during the active project phase.

We offer several options for post-launch support:

  • Ongoing support packages for continuous maintenance and optimization
  • One-off updates for specific changes or additions
  • Training sessions to empower you to make changes yourself

Our goal is to create a brand that works for you long-term, and we're here to support that however works best for you.
